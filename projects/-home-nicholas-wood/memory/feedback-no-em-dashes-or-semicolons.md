Never use em dashes (—) or semicolons (;) in any prose drafted for Nick. Applies to cover letters, emails, applications, recruiter notes, LinkedIn copy, any prose-shaped writing. Replace with periods (split into two sentences), commas, or a restructure that doesn't need either.

**Why:** Nick's settled writing-style rule. The no-em-dash half is already in [[PRINCIPAL_IDENTITY.md]]; the no-semicolon half was added 2026-06-11 during the PM cover-letter drafting (Tennis Australia / Prezzee / The Onset). He explicitly extended the prohibition to semicolons during that session.

**How to apply:** Before delivering any prose written for Nick, scan for `—` and `;`. If present, fix them. Don't ask permission — this is a settled style rule, not a preference. Hyphens (`-`) inside compound words (e.g. "grassroots-to-Grand-Slam", "fit-for-purpose") are fine. Colons (`:`) are fine. Parentheses are fine. Periods and commas do most of the work.
