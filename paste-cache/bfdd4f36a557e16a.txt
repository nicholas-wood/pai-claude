What you’ll be doing

As a Senior AI Engineer, you'll play a critical role in helping shape Virgin Australia's AI future.

Joining our growing AI team, you'll lead the design, development and operation of production-grade AI systems that deliver meaningful outcomes for our customers, our people and our business. This is an opportunity to work on greenfield AI initiatives, helping take AI solutions from concept through to production while establishing engineering standards and best practices across the function.

Reporting to the Lead Engineer, AI, you'll provide hands-on technical leadership across AI engineering initiatives and work closely with Product, Data Science, Platform Engineering, Security, Architecture and business stakeholders to deliver reliable, scalable and secure AI services.

In this role, you will:

Lead the technical design and development of AI applications, agentic workflows, retrieval pipelines, tool-calling systems and AI-enabled services
Design and build production-grade APIs, integrations, agent gateways, knowledge stores, retrieval flows and AI platforms
Partner with Product, Data Science and business teams to translate business needs into practical AI solutions
Establish and champion engineering standards across code quality, testing, observability, evaluation, security and production readiness
Mentor and coach AI Engineers, contributing to technical capability uplift across the team
Design and implement evaluation frameworks, monitoring, regression testing and quality controls for AI systems
Drive best practice across LLMOps, AgentOps, observability and release management
Support incident response, root-cause analysis and continuous improvement for production AI services
Contribute to responsible AI practices, including evaluation, guardrails, human escalation and governance controls
This is a highly visible role that combines hands-on engineering with technical leadership and offers the opportunity to influence how AI is embedded across one of Australia's most recognised brands.
