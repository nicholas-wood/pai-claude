# LSV Communications Officer

## Summary

Nick's volunteer State Lifesaving Communications Officer role at Life Saving Victoria (LSV). The
role covers three domains: LIMSOC (the LSV operational incident-management system), Operational
Communications (radio network, notifications, callout coordination, interagency pathways), and
LSOCE (Life Saving Operations Communications Executive -- the body that endorses operational SOPs
and communications policy). Nick operates as the volunteer leader who shapes priorities, stewards
delivery alongside the LSV Comms and Capability Systems team, and provides executive-grade SOP
feedback to LSOCE.

## Key Facts

- **Role title:** State Lifesaving Communications Officer, Life Saving Victoria (LSV)
- **Type:** Volunteer leadership role (unpaid)
- **Core platform message:** "Right information, right people, right time"
- **LIMSOC scope:** the operational incident-management system; member identity and awards source of
  truth; central to both operational comms and incident capture
- **Operational Communications scope:** radio network (callsigns, coverage, interoperability),
  pager workflows, callout coordination, interagency notification pathways (Triple Zero Victoria,
  Water Police, Ambulance Victoria)
- **LSOCE scope:** executive review and endorsement of operational SOPs; Nick provides feedback on
  decision rights, chains of command, and notification pathways (e.g. SOP 5.11 PRE Activation)
- **Hierarchy:** SLSA (national) > State (LSV) > Region > Area > Club
- **2025-26 season outcomes:** 674 incidents managed through LIMSOC, 1,330 first aid forms, 241
  major first aids; statewide radio callsign refresh; Ambulance Victoria added to LSV channels;
  frontline map capability built and ready to launch; live RPAS footage streaming into LIMSOC;
  annual report and role reapplication submitted
- **Agents:** scaffold-only by choice -- no agents minted as of 2026-06-02
- **Sub-projects nested here:** `lsv-notifications/` (notification workbook tooling, VBA/Python),
  `linear-tracking/` (Linear to GitHub issue sync, own git repo)

## Role Boundary Distinctions (critical -- keep separate)

- **NOT the SLSA Deputy Chair role.** A separate national governance position held concurrently.
  Work products from that role (e.g. national water safety supervision policy) are out of scope
  here.
- **NOT the LIMSOC National Scaling Strategy engagement.** A separate, completed, paid independent
  consulting engagement with SLSA (project: `limsoc-national-ims`). Its output (national IMS
  architecture, feature briefs, delivery workflow) is directly relatable to this role's domain but
  the engagement itself is distinct.
