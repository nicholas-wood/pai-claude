# LIMSOC Season Priorities 2025-26

## Summary

Nick authored the LIMSOC season priorities proposal for 2025-26, positioning himself as the volunteer product leader pitching to the broader team for adoption before full project scoping. The document frames LIMSOC as an agile, greenfield platform analogous to a startup, contrasted with legacy emergency-services incumbent systems. Themes are simplification, cohesion, and AI innovation. Team buy-in (Strategic Alignment / Extended Standup) was required before individual projects could enter full scoping.

## Key Facts

**Four focus areas with 18 projects:**

1. **Enhance Incident Management** (target: Sep 2025) -- 8 projects
   - Incident categorisation
   - Simplified incident creation
   - Secondary information forms
   - AI incident brief
   - ETA on responding services
   - Prompting on information
   - Notification simplification
   - Incident labels

2. **More Information on Front Line** (target: Sep 2025) -- 3 projects
   - Implied access
   - Patrol map
   - Incident info for ERT members

3. **Volunteer Rostering** (target: Aug 2025) -- 4 projects
   - Team rostering
   - Flexible rostering / shift bid
   - Unit-level admin
   - Sign-off reminders

4. **Data Integrity and Reporting** (target: Oct 2025) -- 3 projects
   - PDF reporting
   - AI assistance on incidents
   - AI assistance on first aid forms

**Structure of each project entry:** problem / proposed change / impact.

**Status at time of authoring:** awaiting team buy-in before full scoping; each project would then enter the 10-phase delivery workflow (see companion note: `limsoc-delivery-workflow.md`).
