# SMLSC Clubhouse Master Plan v0.1

## Summary

Nick's master plan for South Melbourne Life Saving Club (SMLSC) clubhouse improvements, authored as a single source of truth capturing the full wishlist, indicative costs ($TBC pending quotes), priorities, and a candidate renewal bundle. Structured for incremental funding, with a renewal bundle option for upfront-funded delivery. Document status: Draft v0.1, dated 2026-05-05. The board-proposal document (see companion note) supersedes the Must/Should/Could + Renewal Bundle framing; scope is now organised by area for the board.

## Key Facts

**Item ID scheme (stable references):**
OFF-## (office), HAL-## (hallway), FB-## (function/bar), CHG-## (changerooms), GS-## (gear shed), KIT-## (kitchenette), CLN-## (cleaning), PA-## (audio/PA), SEC-## (security), BLD-## (building), EXT-## (exterior), PT-## (patrol tower)

**Priority tiers (master-plan framing):**
- **Must:** PA testing process (PA-02), letterbox (EXT-01), PA microphone (PA-01), fishbowl door security (FB-01), office clean-out (OFF-02), washing machine plumbing (CLN-01), dual-side gear-shed fob (GS-01), office dividing door (OFF-01)
- **Should:** keyless entry 12 points (SEC-02), CCTV 5 cameras (SEC-01), hallway cupboards (HAL-01), security cupboard reconfigure (OFF-03), magnetic door stops (BLD-01), PA tower volume control (PA-03), wetsuit racks (CHG-01), drawer dishwasher (KIT-01), acoustic panelling (FB-02), teleconference facilities (FB-03)
- **Could:** gear shed reconfigure (GS-02), code lockers (CHG-02)

**Renewal bundle (upfront scenario):** OFF-01, HAL-01, OFF-03, SEC-01, SEC-02, GS-01, FB-02, FB-03 -- chosen because they share trades, share access-control infrastructure, or are sequencing-dependent.

**Bundle rationale:** FB-02 + FB-03 together unlock external hire revenue; access-control items share a single vendor mobilisation; doing it as one program is cheaper and less disruptive than years of incremental patches.

**All item costs:** $TBC (no quotes obtained as of v0.1).

**Open questions (as of 2026-05-05):**

- Whether bar fridge / training equipment fobs are wired or battery (drives cost)
- Fishbowl teleconference spec
- Whether OFF-01 wall is structural / requires permit
- Decision: progressive funding vs upfront renewal bundle
