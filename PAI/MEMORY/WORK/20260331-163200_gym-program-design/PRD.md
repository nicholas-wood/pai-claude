## Decisions

- Antagonist supersets on Day 4 and Day 5 for session efficiency (validated by FirstPrinciples - no hypertrophy compromise)
- Abs distributed across Push, Pull, Upper (3x3 = 9 sets/week) rather than dedicated ab day
- Three different ab exercises for variety: cable crunch, hanging leg raise, pallof press
- Rest day after Pull (Wednesday) and after Day 5 (Sunday) for recovery
- Drop set on final lateral raise of Day 5 as shoulder finisher
