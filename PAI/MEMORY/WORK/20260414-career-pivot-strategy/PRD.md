## Context

Nick was made redundant from a SWE role and wants to pivot away from engineering into strategy / digital transformation consulting. Has 6.5 years SWE, time as BA and Scrum Master on secondment, policy/strategy/leadership work in Surf Life Saving (LIMSOC project he loved), independent LSV consulting contract, building AccessBuddy SaaS. UniMelb degree, ~70 GPA. Identified BCG TDA Melbourne role as dream target. Has a potential referral path via sister's friend (BCG project lead, Melbourne). Naturally good at case interviews. Out of work, money has finite runway, fear of rejection causing paralysis.

### Risks
- Indecision burns runway with zero motion
- Comfort-zone trap: defaulting to SWE roles re-traps him
- Single-target fixation on BCG creates false binary (BCG or failure)
- Referral mishandled (too cold, too needy, or no homework done)
- GPA gate at MBB experienced hire stage
- MBA fantasy as procrastination device
- Overinvesting in case prep before pipeline exists

### Plan
Treat this as a portfolio of parallel bets with different time horizons, not one big decision. Aggressive consulting push (referral + recruiters + Tier 2 volume) running in parallel with a controlled SWE fallback that gets activated at a defined runway threshold, not on emotion. Reframe SWE backup as "runway buys you the courage to keep aiming high" not "chickening out."
