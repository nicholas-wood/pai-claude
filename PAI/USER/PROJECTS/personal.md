**Rep scheme:**

- Isolation: 3 x 12-15

**Goals:** Fat loss, lean muscle (back, shoulders, arms, abs), straight-leg toe touch, anterior pelvic tilt (APT) correction, resolve right shoulder impingement.

**End-of-Block-1 working weights (2026-05-19, from 22 photo logs):**
- Incline DB Press: 30-32 kg x 12 x 3
- Flat Machine Press: 77-87 kg x 12 x 3
- Back Squat: 100 kg x 12 x 3
- RDL: 100 kg x 12 x 3
- Lat Pulldown (neutral): 82 kg x 12
- Pull-up: 10/9/6 BW
- Cable Crunch: 35-42 kg x 20

**Abs - 5 exercises, all 2x/week:**
- Push + Upper: Decline Weighted Situp, Cable Crunch, Pallof Press
- Pull + Shoulders & Arms: Captains Chair, Ab Roller
- Legs: no ab block

**Cable Crunch is non-negotiable.** Must appear in ab block at least 2x/week. It is a default add whenever ab exercises are discussed.

**APT-aware cueing:** Decline Situp and Captains Chair are hip-flexor dominant without correct form. Cues: PPT before each rep (Decline Situp), pelvis-to-ribs not knees-to-chest (Captains Chair). Swap to kneeling Cable Crunch / Ab Roller if form cannot be felt. Pallof Press and Ab Roller are APT-neutral - no cueing concern.

**Daily mobility (8-12 min post-workout):**
1. APT fix (Couch Stretch, Glute Bridge with PPT, Side-Lying Scissor Kicks, Dead Bug)
2. Posterior chain / toe-touch (Standing Forward Fold, Single-Leg Hamstring Stretch, Calf Stretch)
3. Day-specific add (wall slides, T-spine, hip work, shoulder wash-down - varies by session)

**Active blocker:** Right shoulder impingement - flagged twice in Block 1. Constrains pressing load progression. Rehab work (banded ER, wall slides, scap pull-ups, Y-T-W) should have preceded heavier pressing. No rotator cuff / scap strengthening yet in main program.

**Open backlog (red-teamed 2026-05-20):**

- Stay BW on pull-up until 4x8 BW achieved before adding belt
- Ab volume ~24 sets/week, above 8-16 optimum - drop to 1 ab exercise/session if recovery suffers
- No deload between blocks - consider 50% load week 1 of Block 2 as familiarisation
- Conditioning not addressed - fat-loss goal needs 2x zone-2 cardio (Wed/Sun) or diet-only acceptance
- Tempo (3010) on heavy compounds may slow strength progress - consider dropping on top sets
- Session length may reach 75-85 min - consider moving mobility to separate evening block

**Progression rule:** Advance load/rep scheme only when top of rep range is consistently hit across all working sets. This is what triggered Block 2 from Block 1.
