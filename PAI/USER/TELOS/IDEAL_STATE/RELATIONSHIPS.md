# Ideal State — Relationships

> Where I want the people around me to be. The "you and others" leg of the three-betweens model.

## Family (number one)
- A wife and kids. They are my number one. I want to be fully present and available for them.

## Friends
- Friends I can rely on, have fun with, and be challenged by.

## People I work with
- Work with people I admire and who push me to be better.

## Community
- Engaged in a community built on giving back to others, alongside like-minded people.
- That sense of purpose in community is very valuable to me. I believe you get more out of it than you put in.
