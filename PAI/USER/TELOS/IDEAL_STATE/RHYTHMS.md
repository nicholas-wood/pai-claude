# Rhythms

> The recurring cadences that keep life organised and moving. Rhythms are standing behaviours, not projects. The DA runs them; routine specs live in `USER/Routines/`.

## Operating cadence

- **Daily top-five** - weekday morning, the DA synthesises the five highest-leverage actions across all active fronts and posts to Telegram (PAI's notification channel). Spec: `USER/Routines/weekly-review.md`.
- **Weekly review** - Monday morning, the DA reviews every active project ISA (moved / stalled / unblocked), updates Criteria and Decisions, ages open loops, manages the agent roster, and sets the week's three to five priorities. Spec: `USER/Routines/weekly-review.md`.

## Principle

Orchestration is the DA's standing job, not a separate project. Synthesise, do not enumerate. The PROJECTS index is the consolidated view; each ISA is the per-project record; this rhythm keeps them current.
